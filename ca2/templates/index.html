Instructions

Aim of the Game

  • Use the arrow keys to move the player around.
  • Get to the bottom of the level by avoiding the enemies chasing you and going through the gaps in the walls.
  • If you are caught by one of the enemies, the game will end.
  • Once you reach the finish point at the bottom right corner, you can advance to the next level.
  • When you complete the final level, you have completed the game.
  • The game can be paused by pressing the Escape key.

Treasures

  • As you move throughout the level, you can claim treasures.
  • When you claim a treasure, your score will increase by 10.
  • You also have a chance of gaining an inventory item or power-up.

Items

  • Items can be either weapons or defensive items.
  • Weapons, such as a Gun, allow you to attack an enemy and kill them.
  • When you have a weapon in your inventory, you can fire to the left by pressing the A key, or to the right by pressing the D key.
  • The weapon will be lost after a single use.
  • Defensive items, such as a Shield, allow you to take a hit from an enemy and survive.
  • When you have a defensive item in your inventory, it will automatically be used when an enemy catches you.
  • The defensive item will be lost after a single use.

Power-ups

  • Power-ups give you a certain advantage for a limited time, such as freezing the enemies or allowing you to move through walls freely.
  • They will expire after 5 seconds.

Cheats

  • Cheats give you a certain advantage (the same as the power-ups), and can be toggled by pressing a certain key.
Key Cheat
` (Backtick) Freeze Enemies
# (Number Sign) Move Through Walls
  • Note that if you use cheats, your game result will not count towards your stats or leaderboard position.

Results and Stats

  • Every time the game ends, the result, including your score and the number of levels completed, is stored.
  • Your recent results can be seen on the right of the screen.
  • You can clear your results by pressing the Delete Results button. Be aware that this will delete all your results, and reset your stats and leaderboard positon.
  • If you are logged in, your Stats will be shown on the right of the screen, and will update each time the game ends. You will also be listed on the Leaderboard.
  • When you login or register, you will be asked if you would like to add the game results stored in the current session to your account.
